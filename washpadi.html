Mobile App Consumer 2025

Washpadi

A laundry ordering service designed across three surfaces — a customer-facing mobile app, a vendor management app, and an internal admin platform.

Overview

Washpadi is an on-demand laundry service that connects customers with local laundry vendors. The product spans three distinct user types — customers placing orders, vendors fulfilling them, and admins overseeing the entire operation. Each surface had its own logic, goals, and constraints.

The customer app needed to feel as frictionless as ordering food — quick pickup scheduling, live order tracking, and a history of past orders. The vendor app was a different problem entirely: a lightweight operations tool vendors could use on any Android device to accept jobs, update statuses, and manage pickups without needing technical expertise.

The admin platform tied everything together — giving the Washpadi team visibility into orders, vendor performance, disputes, and platform health. Designing across all three surfaces required a shared design language while maintaining the right cognitive load for each audience.
