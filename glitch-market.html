Glitch Market

An AI-powered prediction market platform where users stake on real-world and on-chain outcomes, with AI agents generating market signals and resolving disputes.

Overview

Glitch Market sits at the intersection of AI and decentralized finance — a prediction market where AI agents don't just assist users, they actively participate as market makers and resolution validators. The design needed to make a genuinely complex system feel legible and exciting.

The key tension: prediction markets attract both casual users betting on pop culture events and sophisticated traders analyzing on-chain signals. The interface had to serve both, with completely different information densities available at different levels of engagement.

I designed the market creation flow, the live betting interface with real-time probability curves, the AI signal feed, and the resolution review screens. The visual language leans into controlled chaos — glitch aesthetics, data noise, neon on dark — to reinforce the brand identity.
