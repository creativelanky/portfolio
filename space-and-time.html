Dreamspace

A Web3-native data platform that brings verifiable, AI-powered analytics to smart contracts and on-chain applications at scale.

Overview

Space and Time is building the data layer for Web3 — a decentralized data warehouse that lets smart contracts query off-chain data with cryptographic proof. The product needed to speak to two very different audiences: developers who need SQL-like query interfaces, and business stakeholders who need dashboards they can actually understand.

The design challenge was unifying these two audiences into a single product without dumbing down for either. I designed a layered information architecture that puts simple data summaries front and center, with technical query builders and schema explorers a click deeper.

I also worked on the developer docs experience and the API key management flows — critical for the technical audience who would spend the most time in the product.
